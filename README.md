## Overview:
This is designed to help HKDSE ICT students master the concept of Database Normalisation. It transforms abstract database theory into a visual, step-by-step interactive learning experience.

## How to Use This App:

Explore the Concept: Use the "Control Panel" on the left to step through the stages of normalisation, from Unnormalized Form (UNF) up to Third Normal Form (3NF). Watch how the data tables split and transform dynamically on the right.

Bilingual Learning: Click the language toggle at the top right to switch between English and Traditional Chinese. When using the Chinese interface, key terms include their English equivalents in brackets to help you familiarize yourself with examination terminology.

Deepen Understanding: Scroll down to explore common misconceptions, key takeaways, and critical thinking prompts ("Think & Link") that bridge normalisation with real-world database design.

Test Yourself: Use the interactive "Self-Assessment" quiz at the bottom to evaluate your understanding. Immediate feedback is provided for each answer.
